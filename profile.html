Company Profile

  • Who are we?
    Roseline Zigomo and Tendayi Musarira bring a combined 48 years of professional experience in the legal fiel to their clients- both local and international. Established as the result of a merger between Zigomo Legal Practitioners and Musarira Law Chambers, the firm offers a full suite of legal services

We Value Our Clients

The firm holds the belief that good clients management amounts to sustainable business practice. Therefore:

  • We seek to continually upskill through actively participating in professional development through many fora and the Law Society of Zimbabwe's learning and development curriculum.

  • We prioritise good communication practice, good facilitation skills, strong written and verbal communication skills in the English Language.

  • We Believe that as officers of the Court we must conduct our work in a manner that preserves the noble profession of the law for good of the client and the firm.

  • We work to maintain good relationships with Government support services such as the staff in the Court registries, the Sheriff's office. Companies and intellectual Property Registries, the Registrar of Deeds, local authorities and Master's office.

  • Established virtual communication strategies.
