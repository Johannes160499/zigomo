About Our Firm

"Justice consists in doing no injury to men;
decency in giving them no offence" - Marcus Tullius Cicero

Roseline Zigomo and Tendayi Musarira bring a combined 48 years of professional experience in the legal fiel to their clients- both local and international. Established as the result of a merger between Zigomo Legal Practitioners and Musarira Law Chambers, the firm offers a full suite of legal services namely;

  • - Family Law
  • - Realty Land, and Property
  • - Estate Planning Law and Administration
  • - Commercial Law & Transactional Law
  • - Mining Law
  • - Securities Law
  • - Alternate disputes resolutions
  • - Employment Law
  • - Intellectual Property Law
  • - Criminal and Civil Litigation

The firm also has a local and international track record in advice on the registration of and the defence of or opposition to intellectual property rights; setting up of investment vehicles and businesses; registration and securitization of land and property investment transactions
